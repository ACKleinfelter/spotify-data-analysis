# spotify-data-analysis
Analyzing audio features of popular tracks on Spotify with Cameron Carter.

## Questions
- How does track length affect energy and tempo?
- How does track tempo affect the danceability, energy, loudness, valence, speechiness, instrumentalness, liveness, and acousticness?
- Are tracks with licensed YouTube videos more popular than those with unlicensed videos?

## Findings
- Energy and tempo were not significantly affected by track length.
- Higher tempos correlate with higher energy and valence while middling tempos correlate with danceability and loudness. Slow tempos correlate with acousticness and instrumentalness. Liveness seemed unaffected by tempo, while speechiness seemed higher in tracks with notably high or low tempos.
- Tracks with licesnsed videos had 52% more Spotify streams on average.
